My Story

My academic training was in public health epidemiology research, and I’ve been working in the healthcare consulting industry for the last decade. My experience involved quantitative and qualitative research, program implementation and evaluation, project management, and process improvement.

Since 2020, I’ve done a great deal of soulsearching on a career change where I can use these skills I’ve honed over the years but also incorporating them in a more creative carerer to create visual products. That’s where I found the perfect pairing between my current experience and the product designer career, and have been building the relevant skillsets ever since.
